Introduction

Project-X (unofficial name) is an upcoming role-playing game (RPG). It’s an open world RPG with turn-based battle system. The game is fully developed on Unreal Engine 4.

Every month, there will be new content added to this website (videos, pictures or information) so people that want to know about this project will be informed with details and be able to express their opinion.

The plan is to release a small part of the first chapter (after that part will be at least 90% completed obviously) to the public. Kinda like an alpha version so people can test it and check for bugs, make suggestions about what can be improved and all that stuff.

The Legend of Dragoon is an old J-RPG that was released in 1999. It had some unique features like additions (combo system) within its battle system that made it a unique game for its genre. The quality of graphics and especially the cutscenes were something that someone wouldn’t be able to find in many games at that time.

For more than 10 years, many fans are asking for a new The Legend of Dragoon game (remake, prequel & sequel). Even though there were some plans from Japan Studio for a new The Legend of Dragoon game, it was cancelled for unknown reasons.

  • In which game engine is the game developed?

    • Unreal Engine 4.

  • Why Unreal Engine 4?

    • I’m not the most experienced and best developer, I have worked in 2 projects on Unreal Development Kit (UDK) few years ago so I’m kinda familiar with that game engine since it’s similar to the old one (UDK).

  • How can someone help?

    • I can do everything a game requires like programming, level designing, sounds & music, general assets (like 3D character models and enviromental objects). However that doesn’t mean I won’t accept if you have something to offer to this project. Any help is welcomed! Especially those who have experience with Unreal Engine 4 or with game development in general.

    If you can’t do anything of the above that I mentioned at the beginning, you can contribute to the project by expressing your opinion if you aren’t satisfied with something. Or even suggest something that you want to be implemented on the game in the future.

    After all, I will be providing information every month (that includes pictures, videos (mostly of gameplay and cutscenes) or general information about the direction of the game). You can either post on the topics, on project’s social media like YouTube, Facebook, Twitter or e-mail me.

  • Any goal?

    • YES! My goal is to release the game before the end of the year.

  • Main aspects of the game:

  • 5 chapters
  • Advanced turn-based system
  • No random battles
  • Open world
  • New Game Plus (+)

    • Thanks for reading and being interested in this project!

    You can also check the project’s YouTube channel , Facebook page and Twitter for the time being.
