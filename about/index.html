About me

About

I’m Geo, currently an indie game and sound developer. I have started developing games with Unreal Development Kit (UDK) back in 2012. Now I’m developing with Unreal Engine 4. Most of my sounds are being made with Serum through Reaper.

Though mainly a level designer, I do programming, 3D modeling and sounds (effects and music).

My focus is on innovative games. Main target platform is PC, web and mobile are secondary.
